Art 2022

Intro

For the longest time, I’ve wanted to get into art. Every once in a while, I’d see amazing concept art online and get a brief burst of inspiration. I’d think to myself, “Damn, I wanna make something like that. But then I’d think about all the time and effort necessary to get to a professional level, and I’d give up before starting.

I don’t know what changed, but at the end of last year (late December 2021), I finally found the motivation to get started. I spent a few weeks copying pictures, drawing boxes, and watching Youtube videos. The next month, I asked my mom for her unused iPad, bought an Apple Pencil, and yeah. Let’s see how much I can improve this year.

So far, I’ve mostly been doing exercises/studies and copying reference images. Maybe towards the end of the year, I’ll feel more confident in coming up with my own original art.
